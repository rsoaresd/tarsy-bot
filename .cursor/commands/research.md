# Comprehensive Internet Research

Perform thorough internet research to gather the most current and comprehensive information:

1. **Choose Relevant Sources** (use what makes sense for the task):
   - Official documentation and API references
   - Best practices and design patterns
   - Recent blog posts and articles (check dates)
   - Stack Overflow and community discussions
   - GitHub issues and pull requests
   - Relevant academic papers or technical reports

2. **Cross-Reference When Needed**:
   - Verify important information across sources
   - Look for consensus on best practices
   - Identify conflicting opinions and evaluate them
   - Check for version-specific information when relevant
